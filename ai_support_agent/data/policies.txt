Company Customer Service Policies

1. Customer First Approach
   - Always prioritize customer satisfaction
   - Respond to inquiries within 24 hours
   - Escalate complex issues to supervisors

2. Return and Refund Policy
   - 30-day return window for most items
   - Original packaging required
   - Refunds processed within 5-7 business days

3. Privacy and Security
   - Never share customer personal information
   - Verify identity before making account changes
   - Use secure channels for sensitive data

4. Service Standards
   - Professional and courteous communication
   - Accurate information and updates
   - Follow up on unresolved issues
